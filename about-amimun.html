ABOUT AMIMUN

Amity International Model United Nations is a simulation of the United Nations spanning over three days. At AMIMUN, delegates gain in-depth knowledge of diplomacy, international relations, and the inner workings of the United Nations. Currently, in it's Ninth Edition, AMIMUN has set a certain standard for the conduct of MUNs throughout the Nation with its innovative themes, focus on pertinent issues and stellar level of debate. Through debating issues that confront world leaders and drafting resolutions, one can enhance their critical thinking, public speaking and leadership skills.
Moreover, the combination of the depth of research required and the understanding one must-have of others' views and attitudes, due to the interactions with multitudes of other young minds from various backgrounds, give the students a deep insight into the real-world problems. They then become aware of the causes of conflict between nations which leads them to a better understanding of the interests and motivations of others. Thus, AMIMUN follows through with the Preamble to the Charter of the United Nations: "To practise tolerance and live together in peace with one another as good neighbours."

WHY AMIMUN?

ABSOLUTE COMMITMENT

The Secretariat of Amity International Model United Nations Conference consists of individuals who have consistently proven themselves to be hardworking and knowledgeable. Each person was handpicked for ensuring AMIMUN’20s ideals are upheld and executed with the utmost dedication. Time and again they have ensured the smooth running of all the committees, while simultaneously maintaining the highest level of hospitality. Thus, we know our promise of quality will be diligently followed, guaranteeing an enriching experience for all who attend.

INTERNATIONAL PLATFORM

Amity is made up of a multitude of people, from various backgrounds, interact and work with one another. AMIMUN takes advantage of this, allowing for a huge international platform, where ideas can be propagated, opinions formed, and views widened. Past editions of AMIMUN have been conducted in association with international bodies like the United Nations Information Centre for India and Bhutan (UNIC), Médecins Sans Frontières, and Amnesty International India, and included delegates and dignitaries from Europe. AMIMUN truly is the best place to not only to showcase one's talent but further grow as a delegate and an individual.

CREATIVITY AND INNOVATION

At AMIMUN we have a long-standing tradition- to be innovative. This, we believe is evident, through the types of committees we create, and the wide array of pertinent issues our agendas cover. When delegates themselves begin to deliberate upon our agendas, they too are forced to think outside the box. It is this domino effect, that preserves the originality that is unique to AMIMUN.

BRUSH UP INGENUITY

As the 9th edition approaches, the excellence of AMIMUN enhances. It provides with finest delegates to keep up with the competition. Moreover, this will develop and enhance skills to negotiate, articulate and market ideas. AMIMUN will foster the critical and analytical thought process to spontaneously come up with solutions to some grave world problems.

ENGAGING AND PARAMOUNT MOTIONS

The dedicated AMIMUN fraternity will bring forth the most engaging motions aimed to facilitate the highest quality of debate. It will provide with a truly holistic educational experience and raise awareness. Moreover, the challenging global issues will stretch the general knowledge of the participants while, sensitising them to the intricate obstacles of being a global leader.
