    AUDERE EST FACERE

FROM THE SECRETARY GENERAL AMIMUN'20

Dear Delegate,
It is an absolute pleasure to invite you to the Amity International Model United Nations (AMIMUN'20). The 9th edition of the AMIMUN Conference will be held at Amity University, Uttar Pradesh from Friday, January 17th, to Sunday, January 19th, 2020.

With each passing edition, AMIMUN has witnessed stratospheric growth, setting the bar for MUNs within the Indian Circuit, and an International Base of participants. By following the theme for AMIMUN’20 “ Audere est facere” which is Latin for “To Dare is to Do”, we wish to encourage the delegates to engage in debate that is thought-provoking and will yield forth solutions to the pressing issues in the world today. We believe that actions speak louder than words, which is why when one makes bold decisions, the results are electrifying.
AMIMUN’20 is not only the perfect place to display your debating and diplomatic prowess but also ignite the zeal to dare and create change. This edition, we as the Secretariat, will ensure your experience isn’t just restricted to a committee room but enhances the never-ending process of learning. It is a platform which strives to enable students from all over the world, so they may move towards a future where they are then capable of achieving what others wouldn’t dare dream of.
I would like to take this opportunity to thank our Founder President Sir, our Faculty Coordinators, our Partners and Sponsors for their relentless support for AMIMUN’20, and making this Conference possible. On behalf of the secretariat, I extend a warm invitation to all delegates and hope this conference will be as productive for you as an individual, as it will be for you as a delegate. For any queries or concerns kindly feel free to reach out to us, we would be glad to assist you. Let’s ‘Dare to Do’!


Sincerely,
Siddhant Treasure
Secretary-General
Amity International Model United Nations 2020
