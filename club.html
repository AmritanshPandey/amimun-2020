ABOUT AMIMUN CLUB

We are the MUN Club of Amity University a group of motivated and sincere students interested in the art of Diplomacy and the world of International Relations. We proudly represent Amity International Model United Nation (AMIMUN) conference which is one of the biggest MUNs in Delhi hosted and organised by Amity University. We work hard and train diligently to experience, exchange, negotiate, represent and learn all that there is. Rich with practical experience and lead by seasoned MUNers, we strive to bring out the best in each member of this club and promote the basic values of the United Nations. The talent is there and the future clear: We want to continue our MUN journey into the future with more experience and motivation and belief in our fellow young student diplomats.


    OBJECTIVES:
  • To tap the potential MUNers/debaters looking for a platform to make their voice heard
  • Help the students nurture their skills
  • Create a pool of MUNers for future such conferences
  • To involve interested students into the world of International politics and relations
  • Offer support and workshops to shape the youth leaders of tomorrow
  • Promote the concept of MUNs and engage more and more students in this activity

    FUNCTIONS:
  • The MUN Club will offer training sessions which will regularly take place during the academic semester
  • To give newcomers a chance to get an insight into MUNing and to learn the procedures and basic skills that are needed to successfully participate in MUN conferences
  • To form a base of MUNers for future AMIMUN conferences both as delegates and secretariat members
  • To help the students participate in other MUN conferences by providing the required training

Message from the President

Welcome to Amity International Model United Nations Club!
Cognizant of the Amity vision towards holistic and overall development, the AMIMUN Club is part of the continuous effort to accomplish this goal. We seek to produce leaders of the highest order, by means of engaging them with MUNs as a tool for self-development.
MUNs over the past decade have managed to transform into a mainstream extra-curricular activity. The primary reason behind this widespread acceptance is the amount of rigour involved; as MUNs are not only verbal debates but a clash of core ideas and their application to the real world. A peculiar aspect of MUNs is the focus on actionable change, something I believe is especially important as it lays a clear focus on problem-solving instead of abstract, principled debate. This focus on problem-solving is what sets MUNs apart from other activities since MUNs actively incentivizes the creation of a wide knowledge base and develops clarity of action. I believe MUNs to be intrinsically valuable as an activity for any student looking to improve their public speaking skills, knowledge base or their approach towards solving real-world problems.
The AMIMUN Club takes up the initiative to promote the culture of MUNing within the confines of Amity University, by actively engaging students via means of Training Sessions and Intra-MUNs alike. Possessing one of the largest student member bodies, the AMIMUN Club has grown leaps and bounds since its inception in 2012. Not only is this stellar growth a sign of MUNs being accepted widely among the student body, but also of a successful effort to enrich student minds by imbibe the values and ideals of MUNs into the student population. We, at the AMIMUN club, will continue to strive towards perfection and are proud to be at the forefront of this mission


Regards,
Siddhant Treasure
President

Message from the Vice-President

Greetings,


As the official MUN Club of Amity University, we are proud to cultivate and promote the burgeoning culture of MUNs within Campus. Over the past few years, AMIMUN has managed to train and motivate eminent student MUNers who have since garnered Awards and Recognition not just as delegates but also as Executive Board to multiple Conferences.
At the AMIMUN Club, we believe in guiding and mentoring our fellow students and peers in the Art of MUNing. Since my maiden year in college, the club has been extremely successful in motivating the student body to take the initiative to take up MUNing through a series of training sessions and seminars.
Led by seasoned MUNers, we strive to bring out the best within individuals, and not only cultivate their growth as competitive MUNers, but also as individuals who are aware of their social-political environs. We wish to continue this journey of growth and motivation, with a strong belief in the ideals of the United Nations.


Regards,
Aakarsh Srivastava

Message from the Member Secretary

Greetings,


We at the Amity University Model United Nations Club, are a set of debaters, who believe that an activity like MUNs, bring about a tangible change, which is shaping our leaders of the future. We believe in the power of student discourse being the solution to the problems which plague us today. I am very proud to be the Member Secretary of one of the largest MUN Clubs in the country, with students of all domains, spending their time working on problems which are necessary to be acted upon.
