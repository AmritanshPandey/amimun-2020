Welcome to the Amity International Model United Nations Club



Since its inception in 2011, AMIMUN has been regarded as one of the finest MUN Conferences, in India and abroad, with its exemplary themes and concepts. Yearly themes such as ‘Turning Grey to Green’, ‘Youth Empowerment’ and ‘Humanity Before Mankind’ has given the participating delegates an opportunity to develop a perspective and gain knowledge about issues which they won't learn in real life.
And this year, with ‘Audere est facere’, which means ‘To Dare Is To Do’, we certainly established a theme that makes an individual even more inclined to take a leap forward, to risk and to believe in what they wish to achieve. It tells them to not only dwell on their ideas but to implement them because only those who dare to try, make it.
Model UN has been an integral part of my overall growth as a person and above all, the HUMANITARIAN that I am today. It has taught me how to excel through real life hardships and challenges with the help of good communication skills, genuine diplomacy and has even taken a part in enhancing my leadership potential. The history of the formation of the UN attracted me from the very beginning and Model UN was a platform introduced to me in my first year of college as a path to learn even more.
